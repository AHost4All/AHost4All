Report Abuse

Use this form if you locate any abuse on A Host 4 All servers related to malware, scams, phishing, spam or similar. Alternatively, you can email us at abuse@ahost4all.com

Send

How we address the abuse complaints?

  • Our abuse department will review each case and take necessary action depending on the situation.
  • In case a website was compromised or hacked, we will send a warning to the owner. If he fails to respond, a suspension will be issued.
  • In case the form of abuse is deliberate, the website and all related services will be suspended immediately. This includes such cases as phishing, malware, 419 scams, child pornography and similar.
